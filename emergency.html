🩹 First Aid

Critical First Aid Steps

While waiting for emergency services to arrive

💓

Cardiac Arrest

  1. Call 112 immediately
  2. Begin CPR (30 compressions)
  3. Give 2 rescue breaths
  4. Use AED if available
  5. Continue until help arrives
🩸

Severe Bleeding

  1. Apply firm pressure
  2. Use clean cloth or bandage
  3. Keep pressure constant
  4. Elevate the limb
  5. Do not remove the cloth
🫁

Choking

  1. Ask if they're choking
  2. Encourage coughing
  3. Give 5 back blows
  4. Perform Heimlich maneuver
  5. Alternate until cleared
